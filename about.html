About this demo website

This website is a demo website to showcase the features of BloomReach Experience Manager.

BloomReach Experience Manager is the Content Management System for creating personalized experiences across all channels. brXM’s open standards philosophy makes it the ideal solution for agile development and open integration.

Editors love brXM because it's easy to use and helps them get their work done quickly and efficiently.

Marketers love brXM because they can roll out and manage channels simply - and increase conversion with targeting and personalization.

IT folks love brXM because they can create new functionality quickly and can integrate with other systems in no-time. And it's just fun to work with the latest and coolest technology.
